Brosnan Free English Classes – Application Form

This programme is not like a school English class. It is a practical, hands-on course to help you become an even better carpenter and team member. You will learn the words and phrases you need to work safely, communicate clearly, and share your skills.

Fully funded for Brosnan team members!

What You Will Learn:

Master Construction Talk
Learn the specific English words for tools, materials, and building tasks you use every day.
Understand Site Plans & Documents
Feel confident reading job cards, instructions, and safety forms.
Speak Up at Toolbox Talks
Learn how to share your ideas, ask good questions, and talk about safety with your crew.
Work Safer
Understand safety signs, hazard reports, and how to communicate clearly to keep everyone safe on site.

Why Take This Course?

Build Confidence
Feel good about speaking up and sharing your skills.
Reduce Mistakes
Avoid misunderstandings that can lead to rework.
Become a Leader
The best carpenters are great communicators. This course helps you become one.
