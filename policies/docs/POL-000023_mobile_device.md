# Mobile Device

## 24.1 Purpose  
To ensure company-provided mobile phones and devices are used safely, responsibly, and primarily for work purposes while protecting company data, minimising distractions, and complying with road safety laws.

## 24.2 Scope  
This policy applies to all employees and contractors who are issued a Brosnan mobile phone, tablet, or other mobile device (whether company-owned, leased, or subsidised).

## 24.3 Eligibility and Issuing  
- Mobile phones/devices are issued based on genuine business need (determined by your manager and HR).  
- Devices remain the property of Brosnan at all times.  
- A Mobile Phone Agreement must be signed on issue, acknowledging the terms of this policy.

## 24.4 Acceptable Use  
Company mobile devices must be used primarily for work-related calls, texts, emails, and apps. Reasonable personal use is permitted provided it:  
- Does not exceed normal personal usage levels  
- Does not interfere with work duties  
- Does not incur excessive costs  
- Complies with all company policies (e.g. no harassment, no illegal content)

## 24.5 Prohibited Use  
The following are strictly prohibited:  
- Using the device while driving (except hands-free where legally permitted and safe)  
- Accessing, storing, or transmitting pornographic, offensive, discriminatory, or illegal material  
- Downloading or streaming large personal files (movies, music, games) on the company plan  
- Jailbreaking, rooting, or installing unauthorised software/apps  
- Sharing the device or SIM with family/friends for regular personal use  
- Using the device for personal business or secondary employment

## 24.6 Safety and Driving  
- Hand-held use while driving a vehicle is prohibited at all times â€“ this includes texting, dialing, or browsing, even when stopped at lights.  
- Hands-free use (Bluetooth or speaker) is allowed only when it does not distract from safe driving.  
- The safest option is to pull over and stop in a safe place before using the phone.

## 24.7 Security and Care  
- Devices must be password/PIN/biometric protected  
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately (within 1 hour) to your manager and IT  
- Do not leave devices unattended in vehicles or public places  
- Install all company-required security apps and updates promptly  
- Do not connect to unsecured public Wi-Fi for work data

## 24.8 Monitoring and Privacy  
- The company may monitor usage (call logs, data usage, location where enabled) for business and compliance purposes.  
- Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company devices or plans.

## 24.9 Costs and Excess Usage  
- Excessive personal usage that incurs additional charges may be recovered from the employee.  
- International roaming must be pre-approved; unauthorised international charges will be deducted from pay.

## 24.10 Damage, Loss, or Theft  
- Employees may be liable for repair or replacement costs if damage/loss/theft results from negligence or breach of this policy.  
- Accidental damage may be covered under the company insurance (excess may apply).

## 24.11 Return of Device  
All devices, SIM cards, chargers, and accessories must be returned in good condition:  
- On termination of employment  
- When requested by the company  
- When a new device is issued  
Final pay may be withheld until the device is returned.

Any questions about mobile device use should be directed to your manager or IT support.
