# Theft and Fraud

## 31.1 Purpose  
To clearly state that theft, fraud, or any deliberate act of dishonesty against Brosnan, its employees, clients, or suppliers will not be tolerated and will result in serious consequences, including termination of employment and criminal prosecution.

## 31.2 Scope  
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, subcontractors, and any person working for or representing Brosnan.

## 31.3 Definition of Theft & Fraud  
Theft and fraud include (but are not limited to):  
- Stealing cash, tools, equipment, materials, fuel, or any company or client property  
- Falsifying timesheets, expense claims, invoices, or any company records  
- Misusing company credit cards, fuel cards, or purchasing systems  
- Taking or using company or client property without authorisation  
- Deliberately damaging property to create false insurance or replacement claims  
- Accepting kickbacks, secret commissions, or bribes  
- Colluding with suppliers or contractors for personal gain  
- Removing or destroying evidence of wrongdoing

## 31.4 Zero Tolerance  
Any proven act of theft or fraud is regarded as serious misconduct and will normally result in:  
- Immediate suspension (without pay where appropriate) pending investigation  
- Summary dismissal (termination without notice or payment in lieu)  
- Recovery of stolen money or property  
- Referral to the Police for criminal investigation and prosecution  
- Civil recovery action where applicable

## 31.5 Investigation Process  
- All allegations or suspicions of theft or fraud will be investigated promptly, fairly, and confidentially  
- The employee will be given an opportunity to respond to the allegations  
- Independent investigators may be appointed where required  
- Surveillance footage, financial records, IT logs, and access records may be used as evidence

## 31.6 Reporting Suspected Theft or Fraud  
All employees have a duty to report any suspected theft or fraud immediately to:  
- Your manager, or  
- A Director, or  
- Anonymously via the Whistleblower channel (Section 30)  

Reports made in good faith will be protected from retaliation.

## 31.7 Consequences for Accessories or Concealment  
Any employee who knows about theft or fraud and fails to report it, or who assists in covering it up, will face the same disciplinary outcomes as the person who committed the act.

## 31.8 Prevention Measures  
Brosnan maintains strong internal controls including:  
- Segregation of duties  
- Regular audits and stocktakes  
- CCTV and access control systems  
- Monitoring of fuel cards, purchasing, and expenses  

All employees must cooperate fully with these controls.

Dishonesty destroys trust and has no place at Brosnan.
