# Performance and Feedback

## 7.1 Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that the Company Employees receive regular performance feedback to support their development and align their efforts with the Company’s goals.

This policy applies to all Employees of the Company. 

## 7.2 Performance Review Process

Performance reviews will be conducted annually, or more frequently if required.

The process involves: 

- Self-assessment by the Employee. 

- Manager’s assessment. 

- Discussion and agreement on goals and development plans.

## 7.3 Feedback

Feedback should be constructive, timely, and specific. Managers are encouraged to provide ongoing feedback throughout the year, not just during formal reviews. 

## 7.4 Performance Improvement Plans

If performance issues are identified, a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) may be implemented to support the Employee in meeting the required standards. 

Failure to improve may result in disciplinary action.
