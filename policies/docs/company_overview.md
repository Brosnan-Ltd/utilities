
# Company Overview

## “The Company”
The following Companies are separate legal entities and are referred to as “The Company” for the purposes of these policies.

Brosnan Limited, Brosnan Construction Limited, Brosnan Construction Canterbury Limited, Brosnan North Limited, and Brosnan Trade Staff Limited.

## Our Purpose, Vision, Mission, and Values
In 2010, Brosnan was established with the purpose of making a positive difference and the desire to build a sustainable business that would last for generations. Our shared vision is to restore Confidence in Construction.

### Our Purpose: Making a Positive Difference
Consistent with our purpose of making a positive difference and building a sustainable business that lasts generations, we believe in:
- Building our own talent from the ground up and providing pathways for career development so that people leave better than when they started
- Building and retaining long-lasting relationships
- Developing a diverse portfolio of specialist services 
- Aspiring for excellence through continuous improvement
- Minimising our impact on the environment by:
    - Maintaining buildings to extend their lifespan
    - Prioritising reusing, repurposing, refurbishing, and restoring buildings over building new
    - Recycling materials wherever practicably possible

### Our Vision: Confidence in Construction
To restore people’s confidence in construction, we believe in:
- Managing the risks, we take on and only taking on risks we can manage
- Fostering a commitment to careers in construction
- Building people and teams on a foundation of trust and integrity
- Offering holistic solutions that consider the bigger picture
- Communicating clearly, openly, and often
- Providing a high-value bespoke service
- Doing it once, doing it right, and welcoming opportunities to improve
- Leveraging our expertise to challenge the status quo
- Advocating for the right procurement model for each project

### Our Mission
We seek to understand and deliver on the unique needs of each client. In doing this, we continually look for opportunities to improve. We work hard to develop relationships built on trust and consistency.

### Our Values
Taking Ownership, Developing Solutions, Building Relationships.

## About Brosnan
Brosnan remediates, upgrades, refurbishes, and maintains existing multi-unit residential and non-residential buildings. We begin with the customer’s needs and create effective, tailored solutions for each project. Brosnan gives clients the confidence that their most valued asset is in safe hands.
