# Workplace Privacy

## 29.1 Purpose  
To ensure that Brosnan respects the privacy of its employees while balancing the Companyâ€™s legitimate business interests, legal obligations, and the need to maintain a safe and productive workplace.

## 29.2 Scope  
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, visitors, and any personal information collected, stored, used, or disclosed by Brosnan. It covers information collected both during and outside working hours where it relates to the employment relationship.

## 29.3 Commitment to Privacy  
Brosnan complies with the Privacy Act 2020 and will only collect, use, store, and disclose personal information for lawful purposes connected with employment and business operations.

## 29.4 What Personal Information We Collect  
We may collect (but are not limited to):  
- Contact details, emergency contacts, and family information  
- CVs, qualifications, references, and employment history  
- Bank account and tax details  
- Health and medical information (only where relevant to the role or managing illness/injury)  
- Criminal history declarations (for certain roles)  
- Performance reviews, training records, and disciplinary records  
- CCTV footage, access card logs, vehicle tracking data, and IT usage logs  
- Photographs or video for ID cards, marketing, or safety purposes

## 29.5 How We Collect Information  
- Directly from you (application forms, updates, conversations)  
- From referees, previous employers, or background checks (with your consent)  
- Automatically via systems (email, internet usage, GPS in company vehicles, CCTV)

## 29.6 Use and Disclosure of Personal Information  
Personal information will only be used or disclosed for:  
- Employment administration (payroll, leave, performance management)  
- Health, safety, and wellbeing (including drug testing or rehabilitation)  
- Compliance with legal obligations (e.g. Inland Revenue, Accident Compensation Corporation)  
- Protecting the safety of employees or the public  
- Investigating misconduct or health & safety incidents  
- Insurance claims or legal proceedings  
Disclosure to third parties (e.g. payroll provider, insurers) only occurs where necessary and under confidentiality agreements.

## 29.7 CCTV and Monitoring  
- CCTV operates on sites and in offices for security, safety, and incident investigation purposes.  
- Signage is displayed where recording occurs.  
- Company vehicles may have GPS tracking for safety and operational reasons.  
- Computer, email, and internet usage may be monitored for security, performance, or misconduct investigations.  
Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company systems or vehicles.

## 29.8 Access and Correction  
You have the right to:  
- Request access to your personal information  
- Request correction of inaccurate information  
Requests should be made in writing to HR. We will respond within 20 working days.

## 29.9 Storage and Security  
Personal information is stored securely (locked cabinets or password-protected systems) and access is restricted to those who need it for legitimate purposes. Information is retained only as long as required by law or business need, then securely destroyed.

## 29.10 Privacy Breaches  
Any suspected privacy breach must be reported immediately to HR or a Director. We will investigate and notify affected individuals and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner where required.

## 29.11 Questions or Concerns  
If you have any questions about how your personal information is handled, contact HR in confidence.

Brosnan is committed to protecting your privacy while maintaining a safe and lawful workplace.
