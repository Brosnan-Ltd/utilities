# Entertainment

## 27.1 Purpose  
To ensure all client or business-related entertainment is reasonable, appropriate, properly authorised, and represents value for money while maintaining Brosnan’s professional reputation.

## 27.2 Scope  
This policy covers all entertainment expenses paid or reimbursed by the Company, including meals, drinks, events, sporting tickets, shows, or any hospitality provided to (or received from) clients, suppliers, partners, or other external parties.

## 27.3 Definition of Entertainment  
Entertainment includes any food, beverage, accommodation, or recreation provided for business development or relationship-building purposes. It does not include normal travel meals claimed under the Travel & Accommodation Policy.

## 27.4 Approval Requirements  

| Type of Entertainment               | Maximum per Person (incl GST) | Pre-Approval Required From               |
|-------------------------------------|-------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Breakfast / Coffee meeting          | $40                           | Manager                                  |
| Lunch                               | $75                           | Manager                                  |
| Dinner                              | $150                          | Director / CEO                           |
| Alcohol only (no meal)              | $75                           | Director / CEO                           |
| Events / Sporting tickets / Shows   | $300                          | CEO only                                 |
| Overseas entertainment             | Case by case                  | CEO + Board approval                     |

## 27.5 Guidelines  
- Entertainment must have a clear business purpose (e.g. building or maintaining relationships, contract discussions, project celebrations).  
- The most senior Brosnan person present must pay and claim the expense.  
- Alcohol is permitted only in moderation and never when driving afterwards is required.  
- Spouses/partners may only attend with prior CEO approval.  
- Entertainment of government officials or council staff requires CEO pre-approval due to bribery and corruption risks.

## 27.6 Claiming Entertainment Expenses  
All claims must include:  
- Original GST receipt / tax invoice  
- Names and companies of all attendees  
- Clear business purpose  
- Pre-approval reference (email or form)  
- Submitted within 14 days via the expense system or Credit Card reconciliation

## 27.7 Prohibited Entertainment  
The following are never reimbursable:  
- Excessive or “lavish” entertainment  
- Adult entertainment or venues of any kind  
- Entertainment solely between Brosnan employees (unless part of an approved team event)  
- Personal celebrations (birthdays, weddings, etc.)  
- Political donations or fundraising events

## 27.8 Receiving Entertainment or Gifts from Others  
Refer to the Receiving & Giving of Gifts Policy (Section 37). Any entertainment received valued over $150 must be declared to your manager and recorded in the Gifts & Hospitality Register.

## 27.9 Consequences of Breach  
Unauthorised or inappropriate entertainment expenses will not be reimbursed and may result in disciplinary action.

Any questions regarding entertainment should be directed to your manager or the Finance team before the event.
