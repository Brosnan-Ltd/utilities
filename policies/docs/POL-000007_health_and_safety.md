# Health and Safety

We are committed to making a positive difference, and this applies to providing a safe, healthy, and productive work environment for workers, contractors, clients, customers and visitors.

Our goal is simple â€“ send everyone home safely every night. This goal extends to ensuring our operations do not place the local community at risk of injury, illness, or property damage. Our commitment will be communicated to all workers and contractors.

## 8.1 We will:

- Use safe systems of work as outlined in the Health, Safety and Environment Management Plan (HSE Management Plan).
- Involve Directors by allocating health and safety related key performance indicators to them.
- Systematically identify and assess hazards and risks in the workplace.
- Report and investigate incidents and implement systems and practices to prevent recurrence.
- Ensure compliance with legislation, safe work instruments, codes of practice, standards, relevant industry guidelines, and safe work practices.
- Regularly review our health and safety system as part of our goal of continual improvement.
- Maintain industry accreditation.
- Provide information to and consult with workers, contractors, and customers to inform them of health and safety issues relevant to our operations.
- Ensure products, plant and equipment are safe and maintained and,
- Establish measurable objectives and targets.

## 8.2 Management is responsible for:

- Following the HSE Management Plan.
- Meeting KPI requirements in relation to key health and safety leading indicators.
- Following our HSE policies and procedures, and applicable laws and regulations.
- Providing and maintaining workplaces in a safe condition.
- Supporting a safe return to work for injured workers where possible.
- Supporting continuous improvement and consultation.
- Training workers and line managers in the competent performance of their assigned tasks and,
- Effectively allocating our resources to meet our health and safety commitments.

## 8.3 Workers are responsible for:

- Following our policies, procedures, and systems of work.
- Reporting all known or observed hazards to their immediate supervisor or manager.
- Speaking up if they believe the task to be unsafe.
- Actively supporting our health and safety program and,
- Contributing ideas to make our workplaces safer and more productive.
