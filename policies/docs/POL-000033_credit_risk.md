# Credit Risk Policy

## 34.1	Purpose and Scope

To govern credit risk for the Company and protect against any unwarranted customer or counterparty credit exposures; maintain credit risk at a manageable level; and identify and avoid a material credit failure (of a significant value), the Company procedure for managing credit risk must be followed.
