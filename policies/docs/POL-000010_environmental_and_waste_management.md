# Environmental and Waste Management

## 11.1 Purpose and Scope

The Company is committed to protecting the environment and managing waste in a responsible manner. This policy applies to all Employees, contractors, and sites.

## 11.2 Environmental Commitment

The Company will:

- Comply with all environmental laws and regulations.
- Minimize environmental impact by reducing waste, recycling, and using sustainable materials.
- Promote environmental awareness among Employees.

## 11.3 Waste Management

All waste must be disposed of in accordance with local regulations.

- Separate recyclable materials.
- Use designated bins for different types of waste.
- Report any environmental incidents immediately.

## 11.4 Training

Employees will receive training on environmental and waste management practices.

## 11.5 Breaches

Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action.
