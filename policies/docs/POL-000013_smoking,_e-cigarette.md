# Smoking and E-cigarette

## 14.1 Purpose and Scope

The Smoke-free Environments Act 1990 requires all workplaces to be smoke-free. The purpose of this policy is to ensure a healthy, safe, and comfortable environment for all employees, contractors, visitors, and clients. This policy applies to all Company premises, vehicles, and events.

The policy covers smoking of tobacco products, e-cigarettes, vaping devices, and any other similar products.

## 14.2 Policy Statements

- Smoking and vaping are prohibited in all indoor areas, including offices, workshops, break rooms, and restrooms.
- Smoking and vaping are prohibited in all Company vehicles.
- Smoking and vaping are prohibited within 5 meters of any building entrance, window, or air intake to prevent second-hand smoke from entering the building.
- Designated smoking areas may be provided in outdoor locations, away from buildings and high-traffic areas, where local regulations allow. These areas will be clearly marked.
- Employees must not smoke or vape during working hours except during designated breaks and only in designated areas.
- Employees are responsible for disposing of cigarette butts and vaping waste properly in provided receptacles to maintain cleanliness and environmental standards.

## 14.3 Compliance and Enforcement

All employees, contractors, and visitors are expected to comply with this policy. Managers are responsible for enforcing the policy and addressing any violations.

Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, in accordance with the Company's Disciplinary Policy.
