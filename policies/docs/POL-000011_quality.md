# Quality

We are committed to making a positive difference, and this applies to providing high-quality services that meet the expectations of our clients and customers. Our standard will be delivered and maintained by our workers, contractors and suppliers.

We place a premium on excellence of service and achieve this through understanding our client’s expectations and continuous improvement. We clearly appreciate and respect today’s market forces that mandate delivery of quality services and products.

## 12.1 We will:

- Use an integrated quality management system to ensure the quality of our services are maintained at all our operations
- Focus quality performance measures on lead indicators
- Maintain our quality program in accordance with respective legislative requirements
- Encourage a partnership relationship with our clients for the delivery of our quality program
- Encourage team problem-solving at all levels to identify and implement work practices that continually improve our standards and productivity
- Carryout out monthly quality procedure reviews on each project and provided our project teams with continuous improvement actions
- Report and investigate defects, and improve systems and practices to prevent recurrence
- Communicate lessons learnt arising from quality event investigation to all our staff
- Provide quality system information to workers, contractors, suppliers, customers and clients
- Review the quality objectives and quality policy for continued suitability
- Establish zero defects at practical completion target for each project
- Continually provide the highest quality products and services
- Identify and solve problems to avoid compromising the quality of our services
- Understand our client’s goals and embrace them and
- Provide ongoing training and resources to advance the skills of our workers

## 12.2 Management is responsible for:

- Meeting KPI requirements in relation to key quality leading indicators
- Following our quality policies and standards
- Engaging with the client at all stages of the project
- Involvement in the development, promotion and implementation of quality procedures
- Training staff in the competent performance of their assigned duties and
- Effectively using our resources to meet our quality commitment

## 12.3 Workers, Contractors and Suppliers are to:

- Follow our policies, procedures and standards
- Report all known or observed quality issues to their immediate supervisor or manager
- Actively support our quality program and
- Contribute ideas which improve our service and product quality
