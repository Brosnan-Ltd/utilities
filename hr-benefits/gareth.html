Professional Development
Study Support
Course fee reimbursement to support your learning journey. We cover 50% of course fees, reimbursed after successful completion (excluding materials, books and admin fees).
How to apply:
  1. Speak with your manager to confirm their support.
  2. Go to myToolkit > Documents > Catalogue > Tertiary Study Request
  3. Select “Assign Form” > “Assign to Me”.
  4. Complete and submit the form to HR, who will manage the request.
Professional Membership
We fund one career-related professional membership annually. A fixed amount per employee has been set aside to support this initiative.
How to apply:
  1. Speak with your manager to confirm their support.
  2. Go to myToolkit > Documents > Catalogue > Professional Membership Application
  3. Select “Assign Form” > “Assign to Me”.
  4. Complete and submit the form to HR, who will manage the request.
